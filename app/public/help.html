FAQs

  1. How do I upload a PDF to ChatPDF?
    To upload a PDF to ChatPDF, simply click on the "Get Started" button on the homepage. Then click on the "Upload" button, select the PDF file from your device and click "Open" to start the upload process.
  2. Is ChatPDF compatible with all devices and operating systems?
    ChatPDF is designed to be compatible with a wide range of devices and operating systems, including Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android. You can access ChatPDF through your web browser on desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile devices.
  3. How secure is my data on ChatPDF?
    We take the security and privacy of your data seriously. ChatPDF uses encryption and other security measures to protect your documents and personal information. Your files are only accessible to you and authorized users you choose to share them with.
